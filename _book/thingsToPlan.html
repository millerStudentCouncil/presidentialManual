Some things to plan for

Really soon, you are going to have a lot of things to do. To help you out, I have created a list of upcoming events and some things to plan for.

Here we go...

  • Over the summer
    • You will have ~3 officer meetings that usually last for 3 hours each. That's the place where you will discuss and start planning for events that happen early in the school year.
  • Veterans Day
    • You get to give a speech introducing Veterans Day. Yaay!
    • Contact Ms. Gasparik and setup a meeting with her to discuss what you need to do.
      • Your Vice-President needs to do this too
    • Mr. Headley can share a folder of past president speeches. It contains the speech I gave, so you can take a look at it for reference
  • The first few projects
    • Spirit Days/Lunchtime Activities
    • First Dance
    • TechTalks
      • This is a project that I started last year and has become a popular event at Miller. Jason Dong (also an officer) has been trained by me to lead this project. Make sure that all of the TechTalk dates are planned over the summer and speakers are contacted. If you need ideas for speakers, you can contact me.
  • Communication Speech
    • Usually, someone in Leadership (it was me this year), gives a presentation on communication to the Leadership class. It will cover topics like how to write a formal email and the different options to spread the word about projects. If you feel like you want to give the presentation, contact a Leadership advisor.
  • Graduation
    • Yeah, I know this is at the end of the year, but it's still important.
    • You get to give another speech. Yaay!
    • The speeches are in the same folder that has my Veterans Day speech.
    • If Leadership students bug you about you asking someone or someone asking you to the Graduation Dance, that's normal. You're not the only person who would have encountered that. :)
