Voting

Every once in a while, you will need to have a vote. The process is pretty simple.

To start a motion, an officer will say: I need a motion to approve (insert topic)

Then, a homeroom rep (or in the case of an officer meeting an officer) will say: I second that

At this point, a vote needs to be taken. You will say All in favor say aye, and get and idea of how many people said "aye". Then, say All who appose say nay, and get an idea of how many people said "nay". If the vote is close, or you need a 2/3 vote for a constitutional amendment or similar, take a vote by raising hands.

If there are enough votes to pass the motion, say Motion passed.
