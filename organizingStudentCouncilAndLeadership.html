Organizing Student Council and Leadership

As president, you are in charge of managing SC and and the Leadership class and organizing them into committees.

One of the tasks you will have as president early on in the school year is to lead creation of the class mission statement. You will have a few days to work with the Leadership class to make the statement. This will give you a good taste of how it is to oversee development of a topic where ~40 students have an opinion. It's fun! ;)
